About


Scottie is a Brooklyn based multidisciplinary artist
specializing in game design and illustration.

Trained in both Fine Arts and Graphic Design,
Scottie grew up drawing characters and worlds
inspired by the video games and comic books of
his childhood.

His art style combines his love of religious
and occult iconography, queer themes, science
fiction, and fantasy.
